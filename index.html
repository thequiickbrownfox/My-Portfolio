Political Bias Detection in News Media
News articles rarely present information in neutral form — choices about emphasis, framing and language shape interpretation. In this project, I built a Retrieval-Augmented Generation pipeline analysing over 1,000 political news articles to examine how ideological perspectives influence narrative structure. The work involved combining semantic search (FAISS), transformer-based summarisation and structured dataset construction to explore how computational methods can assist in analysing political communication at scale.

Which UK Towns are Ageing the Most?
Population change often happens gradually enough that its effects are easy to overlook. Using time-series demographic data from the Office for National Statistics, I analysed how age structure has evolved across more than 300 local authorities. The aim was to understand how generational shifts differ regionally and how demographic change may influence economic resilience, labour markets and public service demand over time.
Skills: Python, Pandas, GeoPandas, data visualisation
